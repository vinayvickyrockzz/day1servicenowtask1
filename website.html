The Source for All Things Cricket

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport that originated in England. It involves two teams taking turns to bat and bowl, with the goal of scoring runs and dismissing the opposing team's players. Cricket is known for its rich history, diverse formats, and passionate fan base in countries like India, Australia, and England.
